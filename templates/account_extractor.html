Quickly extract account configuration info from Gemini Business links and cookies

Method 1: Extract Team ID and CSESIDX from Link

Tip: `team_id` and `csesidx` can be automatically extracted from the Gemini Business link. If you have already extracted cookies from the Network tab, complete this step first to add the Team ID and CSESIDX.
Example: https://business.gemini.google/home/cid/8c7e316a-94ce-46e4-9af4-0d7c58db4914?csesidx=357164620

Method 2: Extract from Browser Cookies

Important: Please run this script on the Gemini Business page (business.gemini.google).

Method A: Automatic Extraction (Recommended)

  1. Open the Gemini Business page and log in.
  2. Press F12 to open developer tools, then switch to the Console tab.
  3. Copy the script below, paste it into the console, and press Enter to run.
  4. The script will automatically extract cookie information and display it on the page.
  5. Copy the displayed result and paste it into the "Cookie Extraction Result" box below.
‚ö†Ô∏è If Cookies are empty: Some cookies might be HttpOnly and cannot be read by JavaScript. Please use Method B to get them manually.

Method B: Extract Cookies from Network Requests (Easiest, Recommended)

üí° Tip: This method can only extract cookies; it cannot get the Team ID and CSESIDX. Please use 'Method 1' first to extract information from the link, or copy them manually from the link.
üí° Tip: This is the easiest method and works even if cookies are HttpOnly!
  1. On the Gemini Business page, press F12 to open developer tools.
  2. Switch to the Network tab.
  3. Refresh the page (F5) or perform any action (e.g., send a message).
  4. Find any request in the Network list (recommending widgetStreamAssist or widgetCreateSession).
  5. Click the request and view the Headers tab.
  6. Find the Cookie: line in the Request Headers section.
  7. Copy the entire Cookie string (it's a very long string).
  8. Paste it into the "Cookie Request Header" input box below and click "Parse Cookie Header".

Method C: Manual Extraction from Application Tab (Alternative)

  1. On the Gemini Business page, press F12 to open developer tools.
  2. Switch to the Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox).
  3. On the left, expand Cookies ‚Üí select https://business.gemini.google.
  4. On the right, find the following cookies and copy their Value:
    • __Secure-C_SES - copy the entire value
    • __Host-C_OSES - copy the entire value
  5. Paste the copied values into the corresponding input boxes below.
